Nurses and Allied Health Training Institute

Hospitals are only as good as the people providing their services. Imperial Hospital knows that quality service is provided by quality physicians, nurses, technicians and other critical staff. Aware of this, our hospital has founded the Nurses and Allied Health Training Institute. This is a full-fledged nurses’ training institute, providing a four (4) year graduate degree for nurses and 3 years’ course for other allied health professionals. The Institute will have an intake of fifty (50) nursing students per year. The Institute will develop twenty-five new Allied Health Professionals per year. The Institute represents the Imperial Hospital commitment to not just serving its need for nurses and other care professional; it represents our commitment to building the base of care professionals in Bangladesh. The hospital maintains a separate nursing staff and student dormitory on its campus with a capacity of 270 residents.
